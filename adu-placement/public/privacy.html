Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FrameUpNow (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use FrameUpNow (the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

DataWhenWhy
Email address & display nameIf you create an accountTo authenticate you and save your projects
Saved project data (address you entered, lot size, setbacks, chosen plan, placement)When you click “Save project”So you can access your work across devices
Approximate locationOnly if you tap “Use my location”To center the map on your area
Local browser storageAutomaticallyTo remember your in‑progress session and theme preference

We do not sell your personal information.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate the Service (sign‑in, saving and loading your projects).
  • To remember your session and preferences on your device.
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following providers, who process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and file storage (your account and saved projects).
  • Mapbox — map tiles, geocoding (address search), and reverse geocoding.
  • Vercel — website hosting.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

The Service uses browser local storage (not advertising cookies) to keep you signed in, remember your current session, and store your light/dark theme choice. You can clear this at any time in your browser settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain account and project data until you delete a project or request deletion of your account. Locally stored session data remains on your device until cleared.

6. Your Rights

You may delete saved projects in the app at any time. To request access to or deletion of your account data, contact us at rford@frameupnow.com. Depending on your location (e.g. GDPR/CCPA), you may have additional rights regarding your data.

7. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your region) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy: rford@frameupnow.com
