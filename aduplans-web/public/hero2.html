FrameUpNow manufactures cold-formed steel (CFS) framing to conform to your engineered plans — lightweight wall panels and trusses, delivered to your site ready to raise.

Learn about steel framingOrder a sample

20 good reasons to frame with metal, not wood

  • ✓ Non-combustible & fire-resistant
  • ✓ Won’t rot, warp, or attract termites
  • ✓ Straight, consistent, engineered accuracy
  • ✓ Lightweight — no heavy machinery
  • ✓ Faster to raise on site
  • ✓ Recyclable & sustainable
Your quick guide

8 steps to completing your steel frame home

1

Delivery of your FrameUpNow skeleton

Your engineered cold-formed steel wall panels and trusses arrive on site, ready to raise.

2

Site preparation

Prepare and level the foundation so the frame lands square and true.

3

Laying out the snaplines

Snap your reference lines to position every wall panel exactly to plan.

4

Installation of wall panels

Stand and fasten the lightweight steel wall panels along the snaplines.

5

Installing trusses

Set the roof trusses onto the walls to shape the structure.

6

Cross bracing

Add bracing to lock the frame rigid and plumb.

7

Tools and equipment needed

A basic tool set is all it takes — no heavy machinery required.

8

Invite your friends

Many FrameUpNow skeletons go up in a weekend with a few helping hands.
